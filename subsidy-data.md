
# Uttar Pradesh Subsidy & Industrial Policies (Organized Summary)

---

# 1. Food Processing Policy 2023

## Capital Subsidy

### New Food Processing Units

* **Subsidy:** 35% of the expenditure
* **Eligible Costs:**

  * Plant & machinery
  * Technical civil work
* **Maximum Limit:** ₹5 Crore
* **Applicable:** All districts of Uttar Pradesh

### Expansion / Modernization / Upgradation

* **Subsidy:** 25% of expenditure
* **Eligible Costs:**

  * Plant & machinery
  * Technical civil work
* **Maximum Limit:** ₹1 Crore
* **Applicable:** All districts of Uttar Pradesh

---

## Cold Chain & Value Addition Infrastructure

### Cold Chain Infrastructure

Includes:

* Storage facilities
* Pack houses
* Pre-cooling units
* Ripening chambers
* Transport infrastructure

**Subsidy:** 35% of project cost

### Value Addition & Processing Infrastructure

Includes:

* Frozen storage
* Deep freezers linked to processing units
* Irradiation facilities

**Subsidy:** 50% of project cost
**Maximum Grant:** ₹10 Crore

---

## Solar Power Subsidy for Food Processing Units

Many food processing units are located in rural areas where power supply is inconsistent.

### Eligibility

* Food processing industries **not located in industrial areas**
* Solar power installations up to **75 KVA**

### Subsidy

* **50% subsidy** on solar power project installation cost
* **90% subsidy** for **women-owned food processing units**

---

## Freight Subsidy on Exports

Since Uttar Pradesh is a **landlocked state**, a transport subsidy is provided.

### Subsidy

* **25% reimbursement of actual transportation cost**

### Coverage

Transportation cost from:

* Manufacturing location in Uttar Pradesh
* To the **actual destination port of the importing country**

### Not Applicable For Exports To

* Nepal
* Bangladesh
* Bhutan

# 2. MSME Subsidy Policy 2022

## Capital Subsidy

### Bundelkhand & Purvanchal Regions

| Enterprise Type | Subsidy |
| --------------- | ------- |
| Micro           | 25%     |
| Small           | 20%     |
| Medium          | 15%     |

### Madhyanchal & Paschimanchal Regions

| Enterprise Type | Subsidy |
| --------------- | ------- |
| Micro           | 20%     |
| Small           | 15%     |
| Medium          | 10%     |

### Additional Benefits

* **+2% additional subsidy** for:

  * SC/ST entrepreneurs
  * Women entrepreneurs

### Maximum Limit

* **₹4 Crore per unit**

### Payment Structure

* Subsidy provided in **2 equal installments**

  1. After completion of building construction
  2. After **50% commercial production starts**

---

## Interest Subsidy

### Eligibility

* Only **Micro Units**

### Subsidy

* **50% interest subsidy**
* Duration: **5 years**
* Maximum: **₹25 Lakh per unit**

### Special Category

* **60% interest subsidy** for:

  * SC/ST entrepreneurs
  * Women entrepreneurs

---

## Stamp Duty Exemption

### Regional Benefits

| Region                        | Exemption |
| ----------------------------- | --------- |
| Purvanchal & Bundelkhand      | 100%      |
| Madhyanchal & Paschimanchal   | 75%       |
| Gautam Budh Nagar & Ghaziabad | 50%       |

### Special Benefits

* **Women Entrepreneurs:** 100% stamp duty exemption anywhere in the state
* **Industrial Park / Estate Developers:** 100% exemption on land purchase

# 3. Technical Upgradation Scheme (TUS)

Designed to help MSMEs adopt **modern machinery and technology**.

### Capital Subsidy

* **50% of capital expenditure**
* Maximum: **₹5 Lakh**

### Interest Subsidy

* **50% interest on machinery loan**
* Maximum: **₹1 Lakh per year**
* Duration: **5 years**

---

# Additional Benefits under RAMP Programme

* **Additional 15% subsidy** for MSMEs upgrading technology
* Preference for:

  * Green technologies
  * Solar or renewable energy
  * Retrofitted devices
  * Women-owned MSMEs

---

# 4. PMFME Scheme

(PM Formalisation of Micro Food Processing Enterprises)

### Capital Subsidy

* **35% of eligible project cost**
* **Maximum subsidy:** ₹10 Lakh per unit

### Financial Structure

* Minimum **10% beneficiary contribution**
* Remaining through **bank loan**

### Project Cost Conditions

* **Land cost not included**
* Allowed:

  * Ready-built workshed
  * Lease or rental workshed
* Lease rental can be included for **maximum 3 years**

# 5. Uttar Pradesh Tourism Policy 2022

## Capital Investment Subsidy

| Eligible Capital Investment | Subsidy | Maximum Subsidy |
| --------------------------- | ------- | --------------- |
| ₹10 Lakh – ₹10 Crore        | 25%     | ₹2 Crore        |
| Up to ₹50 Crore             | 20%     | ₹7.5 Crore      |
| Up to ₹200 Crore            | 15%     | ₹20 Crore       |
| Up to ₹500 Crore            | 10%     | ₹25 Crore       |
| Above ₹500 Crore            | 10%     | ₹40 Crore       |

### Additional Benefits

* **+5% subsidy** for:

  * Tier-2 or lower city destinations
  * Women entrepreneurs
  * SC/ST/Backward Classes
  * Focus tourism destinations

### Incentive Cap

* Total incentives cannot exceed **30% of project cost**

### Other Features

* Land purchase (eligible portion) included in investment
* **PPP projects eligible**

---

## Interest Subsidy

* **5% interest subsidy**
* Duration: **5 years**
* Applicable on bank loans up to **₹5 Crore**

**Note:** Unit can apply for **either capital subsidy or interest subsidy**.

# 6. Dairy Development Policy 2022

Focus on strengthening the dairy value chain.

### Supported Activities

* Establishment of **new milk processing plants**
* Expansion of **existing dairy capacity by 25%**
* Manufacturing units for:

  * Cattle feed
  * Cattle nutritional products
* MSME units for **value-added dairy products**:

  * Cheese
  * Ice cream
* Development of **cold chain infrastructure**

### Cold Chain Facilities

* Milk chilling centers
* Bulk milk coolers
